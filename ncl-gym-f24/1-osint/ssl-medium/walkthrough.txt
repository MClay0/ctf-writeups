Description
    Solve the following questions about the Cyber Skyline SSL certificate.

    Note: if you see references to "BitDefender" in the process of solving this challenge, 
    that means your BitDefender software is intercepting your SSL/TLS connection and will produce incorrect results.

Methodology
    All answers can be found in the certificate details section of chrome
Questions

Answers
